I'm a Ph.D. student at Arizona State University's School of Computing and Augmented Intelligence, with Prof. Arunabha Sen as my supervisor. My research journey is marked by contributions in machine learning and computer vision. Currently, I'm exploring multi-modal representation learning and cross-image domain generalization, with an emphasis on diverse shared label space assumptions across domains.
